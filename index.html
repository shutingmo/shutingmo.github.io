What We Do

Using a web crawler and the Clarifai API, we analyze a website’s images to determine the demographics (race, age, gender) of people featured on a website. Using this information, we calculate scores that assess the website’s overall diversity.

These scores can be valuable in crafting a web presence that is more welcoming to all.
